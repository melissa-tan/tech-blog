## Usage

1. Go to [Tech Blog](https://shielded-citadel-13864.herokuapp.com/)
2. Once the home page is loaded, please sign up for an account and login
	![Screenshot of the Tech Blog Homepage](./public/assets/images/tech-blog-home.png)

3. You will be redirected to the Dashboard where you can see all posts you have created. </br>
	![Screenshot of the Tech Blog Dashboard](./public/assets/images/tech-blog-dashboard.png)

4. Click "Create New" to make a new blog post and input a title and content for your post. <br>
    ![Screenshot of the Tech Blog create new post](./public/assets/images/tech-blog-new.png)

5. From the Dashboard, click on the post title to make edits or delete a post.
    ![Screenshot of the Tech Blog edit post](./public/assets/images/tech-blog-edit.png)

6. Click on home to return to the homepage and click on a post title to make comments on the post.
    ![Screenshot of the Tech Blog comment post](./public/assets/images/tech-blog-comment.png)
